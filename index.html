Merced PCs for Kids

Refurbishing donated computers for low-income kids and families in Merced, California.

Our Mission

We collect old PC parts and whole computers, refurbish them, and provide them to kids and families in need. Your generosity helps bridge the digital divide and offers more opportunities for those without reliable access to technology.

What We Accept

  • Whole desktop or laptop computers (from the last 10-15 years)
  • Motherboards, CPUs, RAM, storage drives, power supplies
  • Monitors, keyboards, mice, and cables

If you're unsure whether an item can be used, please email and we'll let you know.

Data privacy tip: Please wipe or remove personal data from storage devices before donating.

Request a Pickup

If you'd like us to pick up your donation, please fill out the form below. We'll be in touch to schedule a pickup time.

Request a PC

If you or someone you know needs a working PC, you can request one here. Availability is first come, first served, with rare exceptions when someone may have a great or immediate need and may be moved up in the queue.

Please note we work in our spare time and rely on donations, so there may be some time between your request for a PC and receiving one. Thank you for your patience.
